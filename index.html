My Background / About Me
Hello, my name is Zhiqiang Li, but you can call me Phillip.
Starting at a young age of 8, I began my endeavor into the computer world, writing up code in game engines like Unity, Roblox, and Scratch. I fell in love with making and testing different games systems and sharing what I had made with friends.

Fast foward 10 years, I realized I was already on the path of a software engineer, taking more and more classes in high school. Now, I am a college student currently pursuing computer science for undergraduate at Tufts University.

I program in HTML/CSS, Java, C, and Lua, with proficiency in Java and Lua. I mainly focus on back-end, but currently learning front-end. Some of the topics that peek my interest is virtual/augmented reality, AI, game development, and graphical design.
