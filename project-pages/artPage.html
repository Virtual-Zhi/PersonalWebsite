Early Fisherman Tales
After a long day of fishing, the fishermens returns home with his catch. The sun sets behind him, casting a warm glow over the moutains onto the water. The fishermens reflects on the day's events, grateful for the bountiful harvest and the peacefulness of the sea. This was the life of a fisherman, a simple yet fulfilling existence. The sea was his home, and the fish were his livelihood. He knew every wave, every current, and every tide. He had seen the beauty of the ocean in all its forms, from the calmest days to the fiercest storms. I, often, imagined this is how life was like for my ancestors, sailing the seas and fishing for their livelihood. The simplicity of it all, the connection to nature, and the satisfaction of a hard day's work. It was a life that many people longed for, but few could truly understand. It was the life that I had wished for, simple yet fulfilling.

River In Garden
A peaceful scenery was one of my young dreams. Just sitting by the river, listening to the sound of the water flowing, and watching the fish swim by. The sun shining down on me, casting a warm glow over everything. The trees swaying gently in the breeze, their leaves rustling softly. I would often imagine myself sitting by the river, head asleep, listening the nature of animals chirp away. The water would be crystal clear, and I could see the fish swimming beneath the surface. I would feel a sense of calm and tranquility, as if all my worries had melted away. The river was a place of solace, a place where I could escape from the chaos of the world. It was a place where I could find peace and quiet, and just be alone with my thoughts. The sound of the water flowing was like a lullaby, soothing and calming. It was a place where I could connect with nature, and feel a sense of belonging.

Sunset Beach Waves
Another chapter of my life was the sounds of the ocean waves crashing against the shore. The salty breeze blowing through my hair, and the sun setting on the horizon, casting a warm glow over everything. The sound of the waves was like a symphony, soothing and calming. I would often sit on the beach, watching the waves roll in and out, feeling the sand between my toes. The sound of the waves was like a lullaby, lulling me into a state of relaxation. I would close my eyes and just listen, letting the sound wash over me. The ocean became a bridge between my past and present. The waves were the cries of my loved ones from afar where I solely feel by walking into the waters. I stand there where the shore is to show that their message have been received, and that I am here, listening to their stories. Let this place of remorse and calamity be the willow of my past and reflection of my coming future.
